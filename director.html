Importance of Right Salon Training Academy.

Selecting the right training academy for the candidate is a tough and challenging task.

I have found people running pillar to post at the reception doing interaction about their demand and needs. They are particularly known for selecting their academy by considering the price first and looking at the accomdation and conveyance. What is more missing the expertise of the training partner or the training exectutive who delivers the training in the classroom.

With that objective in mind first we looked at how we can help our students to become comfortable. We have made arrangement by certifying good quality lodges with healthy and hygenic meal and food arrangements for our students so that they stay peacefully and that too near to our academy.

I also identified that students lack communication and hence we started training in english as well as translating the content in hindi for them so that they can write the content in their preferrred language since the actualy skill of the candidate is in holding the tools and delivering service.

Master Rashid Ali
